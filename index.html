What We Do

Missoula Herbal Consortium is a not for profit conglomeration of farmers, naturopathic physicians, and health conscious Missoulians geared toward education of the public on the benefits of organic homegrown herbs on long term holistic health. The first annual Missoula Herb Festival will include a variety of lectures, hands on exploration of indigenous herbs local to our area and how to foster growth, and presentations on the health benefits of herbal applications. There will be sample seed packs provided and info given on how to get involved.
